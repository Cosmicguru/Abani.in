About Us

A Leadind Flour Miill In India

ABANI MILL , based at Pathanathitta in Kerala, is one of the leading wheat processing units in India producing premium quality wheat products, Maida, Sooji, Bran, Atta & Chakki Fresh Atta. ABANI started its operation in the early 1980s. Its products had become well accepted by the consumers and rose to fame as the No.10 brand in South India. It has been more than two glorious decades since we had begun our journey and our legacy continues. We have been able to provide an efficient distribution of quality products to our customers because of our relentless efforts to maintain quality. In 1990 , ABANI set up a modern state-of-the-art wheat processing plant and in 2005, we commisioned our new plant and Our premium products are also exported to various Gulf countries.
